About this mod

The Real Monaco map is an excellent mod for Assetto Corsa, featuring excellent streets, roads, and drawings. This mod aims to provide a realistic traffic simulation, which was the only thing lacking from a genuine Monaco experience. The mod attempts to convey a feeling of being in Monaco. The traffic simulation adds a new level of realism to the game. The mod is a great addition to Assetto Corsa, providing a more immersive experience.
