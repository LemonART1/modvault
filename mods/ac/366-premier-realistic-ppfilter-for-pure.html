About this mod

The Premier mod is a post-processing filter designed to enhance the realism of Assetto Corsa. It is currently only available for the PURE version of the game. As the mod is a constant work in progress, feedback from users is highly appreciated. The Premier mod aims to provide the most realistic look and feel possible in Assetto Corsa. It is an excellent choice for those seeking to elevate their gaming experience.
