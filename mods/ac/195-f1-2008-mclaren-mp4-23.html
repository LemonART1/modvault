Experience the iconic F1 2008 McLaren MP4-23, an authentic Formula 1 car from Great Britain. This vehicle features 780 hp, 316 Nm of torque, and a top speed of 320 km/h. Install this mod to bring a piece of F1 history and high-speed racing to your Assetto Corsa game.
