About this mod

This is a free Track Demo that includes the two major spawns, ''Hamburg by 4r'' and ''Harbour Freeroam. The free version does not have Freeroam Access or Extended Highway. In addition, the impending Denmark 1.3 Update will prevent this Free Version from connecting with Denmark via 4r map.
