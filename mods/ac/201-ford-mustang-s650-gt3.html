About this mod

This mod introduces the high-performance 2024 Ford Mustang S650 GT3 to Assetto Corsa. It features a powerful 568 hp engine and 592 Nm of torque, allowing it to reach a top speed of 294 km/h. With its RWD drivetrain and sequential gearbox, the car accelerates from 0-100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds. Weighing 1250 kg, it offers a realistic GT3 racing experience. Please note that this mod requires Content Shader Patch (CSP) to function correctly.
