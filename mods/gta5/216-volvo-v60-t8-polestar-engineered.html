About this mod

Introducing the Volvo V60 T8 Polestar Engineered as an add-on vehicle for GTA V, featuring a base model from Sketchfab. This highly detailed car boasts customized body and caliper paint options, along with tintable glass and working lights. Enjoy a fully functional interior including a customized speedometer, reflective mirrors, and an animated steering wheel with driver's hands. The vehicle also includes breakable glass for a more realistic damage experience. Easily integrate this model into your game using the provided add-on installation instructions.
