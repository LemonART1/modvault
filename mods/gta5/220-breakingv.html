About this mod

This BreakingV mod introduces a dynamic meth crafting system to GTA V Single Player. Unlike other passive meth mods, BreakingV requires active participation and strategic thinking from the player. You must carefully add ingredients, monitor temperatures, and follow precise steps to achieve the highest possible purity in your product. Experience a more engaging and challenging drug manufacturing simulation. Your choices directly impact the outcome of your lab operations.
