About this mod

This mod introduces a selection of jewels designed exclusively for male characters, allowing them to be adorned with gold. It features non-VT textures to ensure visual quality and includes optimizations to minimize clipping or "poking" issues with character models. While not a hard requirement, it is compatible with the "Transmasc Top Surgery for Body 3 AND Body 1" mod. Users will need the "Tutorial Chest Summoning" mod to acquire these new jewelry pieces in-game.
