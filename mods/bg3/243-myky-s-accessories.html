About this mod

This mod adds dyeable accessories in a variety of styles to the tutorial chest in Baldur's Gate 3. The accessories can be obtained by accessing the Cartilaginous Chest inside the Nautiloid, also known as the tutorial chest, using a mod such as Tutorial Chest Summoning. These accessories are primarily usable by tall races with BT1–4, but unfortunately, most of them are not compatible with Githyanki BT2 or Dragonborn characters. The accessories do not have any stats, and it is recommended to use Transmog Enhanced or similar mods.
