About this mod

This mod significantly enhances Wyll's role, providing him greater autonomy in his own story throughout Acts 2 and 3. It introduces a points system, new dialogue options, and over 60 custom-spliced voice lines, allowing Wyll to make his own decisions regarding his pact. The mod also features overhauled Act 2 and 3 cinematic scenes, alongside fixes for vanilla lipsync issues and custom lipsync for new lines. Designed for Patch 8 - Hotfix 36, it integrates seamlessly with companion mods like Hug Your Companions (v2.1.0.0+) and Brightest Star (v2.1.1.0+). Installation is managed via BG3 Mod Manager, and it's recommended to use alongside Be Nice To Wyll and Francesca's Journal.
