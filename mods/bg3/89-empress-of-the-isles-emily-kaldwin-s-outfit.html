About this mod

This mod introduces Emily's outfit for Baldur's Gate 3, specifically designed for BT1 races including humans, elves, half-elves, drow, and tieflings. It provides comprehensive armor options, featuring an outfit, scarf, ring, and boots, alongside distinct outfit and boots options for camp wear. The mod supports TMTS and is non-VT, though minor clipping might occur. Installation requires the Baldur's Gate 3 Mod Manager, and the full outfit can be located within the Tutorial Chest in-game.
