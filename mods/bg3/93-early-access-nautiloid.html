About this mod

This mod meticulously restores the Front Deck section of the Nautiloid, along with other previously unused content and various associated fixes. It seamlessly integrates cut material, including dialogue from Early Access builds, ensuring a polished experience as if it were part of the final game. A notable feature is a restored voice line for Gale, recorded by his original actor Tim Downie. Installation requires loading the mod near the bottom of your mod order, especially after "HT's Early Access Scenes mod," and necessitates starting a new game for full effect. The mod also reverts the Tutorial Chest's location to the starting room on the Nautiloid, as it was in Early Access.
