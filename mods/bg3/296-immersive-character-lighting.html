About this mod

This mod overhauls Baldur's Gate 3's character lighting system to provide a more natural and cinematic appearance. The vanilla game's character lighting suffers from issues such as over-illuminated rim lights, harsh key lights, and unrealistic color temperatures. This mod rebalances the lighting setup to create a more immersive experience. It disables unrealistic key lights and rim lights, improves the light ratio for natural contrast, and eliminates harsh blue casts. The mod also ensures consistent warm-neutral tones across all environments and simplifies the lighting to a single optimized fill light.
