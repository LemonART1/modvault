About this mod

This mod fixes a broken script in the vanilla game that prevents Zethino's love test buffs from being applied. It restores the Dryad's Blessing and Love Protects buffs, with adjustments for increased practicality and visual appeal. The mod offers multiple versions for different gameplay preferences, including temporary, permanent passive, and retroactive passive options.
