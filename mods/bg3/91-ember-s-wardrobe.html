About this mod

This mod introduces a diverse collection of themed outfits to Baldur's Gate 3, ranging from swashbuckler to gothic vampire aesthetics. These new garments are found in a container within your camp chest and come equipped with modest, generic scaling stats, which are not tied to specific classes. The mod offers both default 2K custom textures and an optional 4K texture pack for higher detail, making use of some third-party assets for visual elements. It is generally compatible with other mods and can be safely added mid-campaign, but requires specific in-game steps or PC script extender mods for complete removal to clear custom items from your save.
