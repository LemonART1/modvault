About this mod

This mod adds new hairstyles to the character creation menu, compatible with all body types for various character races. The hairstyles have custom textures and are made from scratch. There are currently 3 new hairstyles with icons added to the menu. The hairstyles do not have physics yet, but they are compatible with different body types. The mod is installed using the BG3 Mod Manager.
