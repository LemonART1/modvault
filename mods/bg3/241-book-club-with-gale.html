About this mod

This mod places a stool in Gale's camp spot and adds over 50 unique reactions from him when you sit on it. Gale's greetings and responses will dynamically change based on your current relationship status and approval level with him. An optional "Meme Edition" is available, replacing these lines with humorous, simplified dialogue. All dialogue uses repurposed existing in-game voice content, ensuring no new audio files are added. The mod is installed with a mod manager and is safe for mid-playthrough installation.
