About this mod

This mod introduces two distinct armor sets along with a matching pair of boots, all conveniently placed within the tutorial chest for immediate access. One version of the armor is designed to fit the standard body type, ensuring broad compatibility. The other version specifically supports the TMTS body type, particularly for BT1 users, catering to a wider range of character appearances. Users can choose the appropriate armor based on their character's body model.
