About this mod

Experience the highly detailed Toyota Camry XV55 3.0, meticulously crafted with high-quality bodywork and an exquisitely detailed interior. Enjoy interactive elements like animated dashboards, pedals, and handbrake, alongside openable doors, hood, and trunk. Included are various skins such as police and taxi, custom wheels, and dynamic damage modeling. The latest update introduces daytime running lights, Lexus-style wheels, a redesigned steering wheel, and numerous visual enhancements for an improved driving experience.
