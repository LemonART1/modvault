About this mod

This mod adds a highly detailed 2015-2023 Dodge Charger to BeamNG.drive. It features a high-quality model, engine compartment, interior, and PBR textures. The mod includes good damage modeling, custom sounds, animated dashboard and pedals, and working lights and mirrors. It also offers tuning options, custom wheels, skins, and 30 configurations for extensive customization.
