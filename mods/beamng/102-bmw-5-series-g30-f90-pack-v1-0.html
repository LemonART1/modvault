About this mod

This mod introduces a comprehensive BMW 5-Series G30/F90 pack, featuring the 530, 540, M550, and M5 trim levels. The JBeam chassis and engine are meticulously based on real-world data, offering a balanced blend of comfort and sportiness. Enjoy a wide range of exterior tuning options, numerous engine settings, and a highly detailed 1:1 scale 3D model of the B58 engine. All parts are interactive, complemented by a clear exterior design, Apple Car Play functionality, and realistic adaptive headlights for an immersive driving experience.
