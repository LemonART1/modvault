About this mod

This mod brings a high-quality Volkswagen Passat B8PS to BeamNG.drive, featuring both sedan and wagon variants across 41 detailed configurations. It includes an intricately modeled exterior, interior, and engine bay, all enhanced with PBR textures and robust JBeam physics for realistic behavior. Experience an advanced hybrid system, similar to the real VW GTE, offering distinct electric-only, sport, and drive modes for varied power delivery. The vehicle also boasts animated dashboards, functional lighting, working navigation, and extensive customization options like window and headlight tints. An added AutoHold system further refines the driving experience.
