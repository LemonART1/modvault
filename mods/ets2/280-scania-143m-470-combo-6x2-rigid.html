About this mod

The Scania 143M 470 Combo 6x2 Rigid is a standalone truck mod for Euro Truck Simulator 2. It features a 6x2 taglift chassis, two engine sounds, and four transmissions. The mod also includes two interiors, beige and brown, and black and grey, as well as six steering wheels. Additionally, there are five optional bullbars available in chrome and paint finishes. This mod provides a realistic and detailed trucking experience for players.
