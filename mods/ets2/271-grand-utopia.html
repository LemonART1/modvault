About this mod

Grand Utopia invites all truck enthusiasts to explore a new virtual world in Euro Truck Simulator 2. This standalone map features a vast territory with many roads and interesting interchanges. Discover meticulously built cities and picturesque natural landscapes throughout your journeys. The map is designed at a realistic 1:1 scale, offering an immersive driving experience. Prepare for endless hours of exploration and challenging routes.
