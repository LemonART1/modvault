About this mod

This mod introduces a highly detailed Scania S650 Eckhardt truck and a Lamberet trailer to Euro Truck Simulator 2. It features a Highline cab, 6x2 Midlift chassis, and a powerful DC16 650 Openpipe engine. The truck boasts an exclusive custom interior with red lighting, a custom salon, unique wheels, and a custom lightmask. It's fully standalone with custom models, and recent updates include fixes for the GPS display, updated textures, and adaptation for game version 1.59.
