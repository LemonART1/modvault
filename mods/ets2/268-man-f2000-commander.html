About this mod

This mod introduces the MAN F2000 Commander, a highly detailed standalone truck for Euro Truck Simulator 2. It features high-poly 3D models, advanced coupling, and support for custom license plates. The package includes ownable trailers, unique wheels, and high-quality lightmasks for enhanced visuals. Experience realistic cable physics, an animated window, and a custom flarepack. A template is also provided for easy skinning and personalization.
