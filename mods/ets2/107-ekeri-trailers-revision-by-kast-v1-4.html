About this mod

This mod offers a comprehensive revision of Ekeri trailers, now consolidated into a single pack for both semi and full trailer configurations. Version 1.4 introduces new accessory slots for marker lights and rear top lights, along with several bug fixes for enhanced stability. Players can choose from various semi-trailer types, including Standard, Step-frame, City, Double, and HCT options. Realistic features such as advanced coupling, trailer lift axle, animated braces, and trailer cables are fully integrated. The mod aims to provide a highly detailed and customizable trailer experience for Euro Truck Simulator 2.
