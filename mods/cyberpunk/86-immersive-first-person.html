About this mod

This mod significantly enhances the first-person perspective in Cyberpunk 2077 by making the player's torso visible, allowing users to better appreciate their character's outfits. It introduces a "Free Look" feature, enabling players to rotate the camera independently without moving their body, which integrates well with zooming, scanning, and interactions. The camera improvements are designed to be unobtrusive, primarily activating when aiming down sights. A crucial dependency for this mod is Cyber Engine Tweaks, where users will also configure specific hotkeys for the Free Look functionality. Please be aware of a known bug where the "Smooth Transition From FreeLook" option might cause characters to get stuck, requiring a save reload and option adjustment.
