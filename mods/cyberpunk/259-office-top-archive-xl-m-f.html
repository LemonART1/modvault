About this mod

This mod introduces an elegant office-style top for both masculine and feminine V characters. It comes in 11 vibrant colors, including opaque and wet variants, with automatic refits for various body types. The bra component can be easily removed using Appearance Change Menu (ACM). Please avoid editing your character while wearing the top to prevent loading issues; use Equipment-EX to fix it if it occurs. This item is designed with photomode in mind, so first-person perspective issues will not be addressed.
