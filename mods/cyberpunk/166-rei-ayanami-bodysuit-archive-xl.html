About this mod

This mod introduces a stylish Rei Ayanami inspired bodysuit, exclusively designed for female V characters. The bodysuit is available in multiple vibrant colors, allowing for extensive customization. It is compatible with various Hyst Body types, including Vanilla, ANGEL, EBB, and other popular options. This outfit is designed to be fully gameplay and photomode friendly, ensuring seamless integration into your Cyberpunk 2077 experience. Easily acquire different color variations of the bodysuit using the provided in-game console commands.
