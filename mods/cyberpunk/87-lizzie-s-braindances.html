About this mod

This mod introduces an extensive braindance catalog to Lizzie's Bar, offering a wide array of new interactive experiences within Cyberpunk 2077. Players can choose from over 300 characters to engage in romance-focused, relaxation-oriented, or social hangout braindances. A key feature is the streaming service, allowing users to enjoy the full braindance selection from the comfort of their own apartments without needing to visit the bar directly. The mod is SFW by default, with an option to enable explicit content upon user request, enhancing role-playing and immersion. Installation requires additional unspecified prerequisite mods, and specific uninstall steps are provided for proper removal.
