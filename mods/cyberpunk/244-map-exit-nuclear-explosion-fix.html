About this mod

This mod provides a crucial fix for a persistent visual bug. It eliminates the jarring "nuclear explosion" flashbang effect that sometimes occurs when exiting the map. Players will no longer be subjected to unexpected visual disruptions during transitions. Note that this mod is incompatible with the "Realistic Map" modification. The fix remains effective and relevant even after the game's 2.31 update.
