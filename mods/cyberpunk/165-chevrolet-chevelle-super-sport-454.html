About this mod

This mod introduces a highly detailed 1970 Chevrolet Chevelle SS 454 to your game. It functions as a completely standalone vehicle, ensuring it doesn't replace any existing in-game cars. Seamlessly integrate this classic muscle car into your collection with full support for the Call menu and Virtual Car Dealer. Experience the iconic power and style of the Chevelle SS 454 cruising the streets of Night City.
