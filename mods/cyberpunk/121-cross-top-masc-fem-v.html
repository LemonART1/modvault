About this mod

This mod introduces a stylish Cross Top designed for both male and female V characters in Cyberpunk 2077. It offers extensive compatibility, supporting numerous popular body mods for female V, including Vanilla, EBBP, EBB, EVE, Angel, Small, and Solo 2.0. Male V also receives support for Vanilla and Gymfiend body types. Enhance your character's wardrobe with this versatile and well-integrated clothing item. Installation can be done conveniently via Vortex or through a simple manual file extraction into your game folders.
