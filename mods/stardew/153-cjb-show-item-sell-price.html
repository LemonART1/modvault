About this mod

This mod displays the sell price of items directly in your inventory or hotbar. It shows the unit price and the total price next to your cursor. The mod is compatible with Stardew Valley 1.6.15+ and works in both single-player and multiplayer. Non-English translations are included, and contributions are welcome.
