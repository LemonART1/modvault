About this mod

This mod offers a complete game recolour, transforming all outdoor tilesheets for a fresh look. It includes custom trees, dig spots, and grass, alongside revamped town buildings designed to match the natural terrain. Players can customize various elements like grass, dig spots, fruit trees, and building appearances via the config file. This mod enhances the visual experience, making Stardew Valley more vibrant and cohesive.
