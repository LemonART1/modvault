About this mod

Transform your Stardew Valley farm with a complete set of medieval-themed buildings. This mod offers multiple seasonal options for all your farm structures, allowing for a charming, rustic aesthetic. Inspired by popular visual mods, it features improved color matching with several recolor packs. It began as a small edit but evolved into a comprehensive re-imagining of farm architecture. SMAPI and Content Patcher are required for installation to bring this beautiful visual overhaul to your game.
