About this mod

This mod replaces the male Wizard, Magnus, with his female counterpart, Rasmodia. It includes a new portrait and sprite, and adjusts dialogue, events, and quests from the base game and various other mods to reflect the gender change. Rasmodia is only romanceable when used with Stardew Valley Expanded or a specific version of Romanceable Rasmodius.
