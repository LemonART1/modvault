About this mod

This mod allows players to kiss NPCs after reaching ten hearts with them. You can customize which NPCs can be kissed and the conditions required. The mod mimics the marriage kiss mechanics, offering friendship bonuses and curing exhaustion. It also includes an optional jealousy system for married players.
