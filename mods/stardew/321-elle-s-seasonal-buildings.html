About this mod

This mod provides a comprehensive Content Patcher pack to replace all farm buildings with seasonal variations. Choose from seven distinct color palettes and three unique building themes to perfectly match your farm's aesthetic. It offers eight different farmhouse styles, optional holiday decor, and configurable wall colors for upgraded farmhouses. Additionally, the mod includes optional recolors for fences, shipping bins, mailboxes, and fish ponds, with the ability to disable any retexture. Seasonal overlays ensure your buildings always complement your chosen map mod and the current season.
