About this mod

Lookup Anything is a Stardew Valley SMAPI mod that shows detailed live information about any object, NPC, item, crop, menu element, or UI element under your cursor when pressing F1, including villager gift preferences, crop growth time, fence durability, animal happiness, and other gameplay data, supports search mode with Shift F1 and controller fallback display, includes full translations and optional customization via Generic Mod Config Menu, works in single player and multiplayer with partial limitations on NPC schedules, and helps players access hidden game data instantly without manual lookup or external guides
