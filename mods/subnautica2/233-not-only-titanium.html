About this mod

This mod overhauls scanner rewards to make exploration more meaningful and rewarding. Previously discovered objects will always grant additional materials, while newly scanned objects also have a chance to drop useful resources. The mod includes adjustable loot rates and scanner speed. It has been tested and works in multiplayer.
