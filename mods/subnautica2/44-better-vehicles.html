About this mod

Better Vehicles mod for Subnautica 2 v0.10.0 improves vehicle performance and handling for Tadpole, Scout chassis, Haul chassis and Seafrog with increased upgrade slots, faster movement speeds, better strafing control, improved inertia behavior, expanded cargo capacity for Haul chassis, reduced postprocessing effects, optional wake booster speed increase, and improved single player interaction adjustments, requires manual installation into Paks ~mods folder and supports modular optional files for customization
